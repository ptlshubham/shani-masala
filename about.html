Mission and Vision of company

OUR MISSION

We strive to bring the best quality masala that is good for taste and health.

OUR VISION

We hope to be part of every dish in every kitchen in India.

OUR VALUES

We use all-natural ingredients and spices to make the perfect masala.

With over 10 years of experience in the food industry, Shana Masala has been one of the leading manufacturers of spices, masalas, and food powders for over a decade.

The company has its manufacturing unit in Navsari and a distribution network spread across India.

Shani Masala products are available at all food markets, supermarkets, and provision stores in Gujarat. We hope to be in every kitchen with our proud masalas because our masalas are made with all-natural products and have no additives or artificial flavors, coloring, or essence, and most of all, all of our products have no Ajinomoto.

We hope to make every dish healthy and special with the best flavors.

Almost every household or cuisine uses several types of masala powders on a regular basis. Masala powder is the ground powder of a full spice mixture, and masala simply means "blend of spices."

These powders are thought to raise body warmth, according to Ayurvedic medicine. That is why Shani Masala brings you the best of spices, made from all-natural ingredients. Each sort of Shani masala powder has important elements that provide the dish it's used in a distinct flavor. The ingredients are briefly roasted in a pan with or without oil or ghee, cooled, and then crushed to a fine powder in a blender or mixer to make masala powder

We strive to give you the flavors your dish need to make it special.
